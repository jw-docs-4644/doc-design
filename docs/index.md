# Doc-Design: Human-powered Document Design

Authenticity matters. Generative AI is great at supporting documentation practices: writing code, moving files, updating structures. But people don't want to read AI-generated text and they are turned off by documentation that reads like "AI Slop," or worse, contains hallucinated errors. 

I am a technical communication consultant with specialization in the education field. I have a PhD in Rhetoric and Scientific and Technical Communication. I teach a range of technical and professional writing courses at Central Washington University. 

I can help your organization leverage tools and systems such as Markdown, Git, and LLM-assisted planning to make your documentation as effective as your code is. 

I can help with

- **Technical Writing Strategy** – Build documentation systems that your whole organization can use. 
- **Tool Implementation** – Deploy documentation leveraging automation technologies. 
- **Training and Support** – Help your team adopt docs-as-code workflows through meta-documentation or training development. 
- **User Analysis** – Learn about the people who use your products through industry-standard tools and practices.
